UNKNOWN

Unknowns are enigmatic and often rare monsters that defy easy classification within the established categories of the Monster Hunter universe. These creatures possess unique and sometimes bizarre traits that set them apart, making them a subject of intense curiosity and caution among hunters and researchers.

Unpredictable Abilities: Unknowns often have abilities and behaviors that are not fully understood, such as Gore Magala's ability to induce frenzy or Shagaru Magala's manipulation of frenzy virus.

Unique Life Cycles: Many Unknowns undergo dramatic transformations or have unusual life cycles that contribute to their mysterious nature.

Rare Encounters: These monsters are rarely encountered, adding to their mystique and the challenge of studying them.

Distinctive Appearances: Unknowns often have striking and unique appearances that set them apart from other monster classes, making them easily recognizable yet difficult to categorize.

Example: Gore Magala, Shagaru Magala
