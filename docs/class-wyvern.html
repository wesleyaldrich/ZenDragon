WYVERN

Wyverns are a diverse and widespread group of monsters characterized by their winged, often dragon-like appearance. They come in various shapes and sizes, adapted to different habitats and roles within the ecosystem. Wyverns are among the most commonly encountered monsters in the Monster Hunter universe, each with unique traits and behaviors.

Diverse Forms: Wyverns can range from the small and agile Bird Wyverns to the massive and powerful Flying Wyverns.

Flight Capabilities: Most Wyverns have wings, allowing them to fly or glide, giving them a significant advantage in mobility and hunting.

Adaptations: Wyverns are highly adaptable, with species suited to various environments, such as deserts, forests, and mountains. This adaptability is seen in monsters like the fire-breathing Rathalos or the venomous Pukei-Pukei.

Predatory Instincts: Wyverns are often apex predators in their regions, utilizing their claws, teeth, and elemental abilities to dominate their territories.

Example: Barioth, Tigrex
