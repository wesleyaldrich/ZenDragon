ELDER DRAGON

Elder Dragons are ancient and immensely powerful creatures that possess extraordinary abilities, often surpassing those of other monsters. These majestic beings are revered and feared across the world of Monster Hunter, as their presence can drastically alter the environment and ecosystem. Each Elder Dragon is unique, with powers that can manipulate elements such as fire, ice, wind, or even more mysterious forces.

Ancient Power: Elder Dragons have existed for centuries, accumulating vast amounts of power and knowledge.

Environmental Impact: Their mere presence can cause significant changes in weather patterns, ecosystems, and landscapes.

Unique Abilities: Each Elder Dragon has distinct abilities, often tied to elemental or natural forces, such as Teostra's mastery of fire or Kushala Daora's control over storms.

Mystical Aura: They exude a mystical aura that sets them apart from other monsters, often making them difficult to study and understand fully.

Example: Alatreon, Fatalis, Velkhana, Kushala Daora
