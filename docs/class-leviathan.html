LEVIATHAN

Leviathans are large, serpentine creatures that thrive in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments. Known for their agility in water and adaptability on land, Leviathans are formidable predators with a variety of specialized adaptations that make them masters of their domains.

Aquatic Mastery: Leviathans are often found in or near water, using their environment to their advantage in battles.

Serpentine Bodies: Their elongated, flexible bodies allow them to move swiftly and gracefully through water and navigate challenging terrains on land.

Adaptable Hunters: With features such as fins, webbed limbs, or even amphibious capabilities, Leviathans can effectively hunt and evade predators in multiple environments.

Powerful Attacks: Many Leviathans can unleash devastating attacks, often involving water manipulation or brute force, such as Lagiacrus's electrical discharges or Mizutsune's bubble blasts.

Example: Amatsumagatsuchi, Lagiacrus
